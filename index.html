Likun Xie

E-mail: xie@mpim-bonn.mpg.de

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn. I completed my Ph.D. at the University of Illinois Urbana–Champaign in May 2025. My general area is number theory, especially problems with algebraic, combinatorial, and arithmetic-geometric aspects. I am also interested in homological questions and local cohomology in commutative algebra. CV
