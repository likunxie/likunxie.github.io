Likun Xie

E-mail: xie@mpim-bonn.mpg.de

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign in May 2025 (Math Genealogy). My research is in analytic number theory, especially its combinatorial and arithmetic-geometric aspects. Here is my CV.
